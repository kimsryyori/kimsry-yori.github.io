Our Story

DREAM Cafe was born from a longing for ‘home’. For us, home is more than the places we find ourselves in. Home is history - the objects that remind us of the sights and smells of childhood - the lucky cat greeting us every day, the warm, nutty smell of a cup of coffee in the morning.


We hope that DREAM Cafe brings you closer to feelings of home.
